 This event is open to all categories of runners.

 Only those who finish the race within the allotted time will receive medals.

 This is a live event, and there is no option for virtual participation.

 Only participants who are registered can join the race using their assigned individual BIB. Transfer of BIB is strictly prohibited.

 Registration fees are non-refundable.

 Only physically fit and interested individuals are encouraged to participate.
