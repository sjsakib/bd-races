Dhaka 25K 2026: Conquer the Capital
Mark your calendars! The Dhaka 25K returns for its thrilling 3rd edition on February 13th, 2026. This internationally recognized race, a proud member of AIMS and boasting a World Athletics-certified course, promises a world-class running experience through the heart of Dhaka.
Ready for the Challenge?
After two successful years, we're raising the bar. The 2026 Dhaka 25K offers a new challenge for every runner, from seasoned marathoners to families looking for an active day out. Choose your distance:
* The Feature Race: 25K: Test your limits on this challenging course and experience the energy of Dhaka.
* 10.3K Fun Run: A perfect distance for intermediate runners seeking a rewarding achievement.
* 3K Family Fun Run: Bring the whole family and enjoy a fun, healthy run through the city. Kids and families of all fitness levels are welcome!
Early Bird Registration: Don't Miss Out!
Secure your spot at the best price! Early bird registration opens on November 1st, 2025.
A World-Class Event:
Dhaka 25K is more than just a race; it's an experience. As a full AIMS member with a World Athletics-certified course, we meet the highest international standards. Join runners from around the globe and discover the vibrant culture and warm hospitality of Dhaka. See what the international media is saying about us! [Link to Media Coverage Here]
What Awaits You:
* Certified Course: Run on a World Athletics-certified track, ensuring accuracy and a professional racing experience.
* Multiple Distances: Choose the race that suits your fitness level and goals.
* Exciting Atmosphere: Experience the thrill of race day with thousands of fellow runners and enthusiastic supporters.
* Family Fun: The 3K Family Fun Run is a great way to get active and create lasting memories.
* Cultural Immersion: Discover the beauty and vibrancy of Dhaka while participating in a world-class event.
Join us on February 13th, 2026, and become part of the Dhaka 25K story!
