The Albatross ULTRAIL is coming — your chance to push your limits in one of the most scenic and challenging trails Bangladesh has to offer. With 33km, 50km, and 83Km of rugged terrain, this is the race that will test your endurance and spirit. Named after the Albatross, a bird that symbolizes freedom, endurance, and the thrill of the open skies, this ultra-trail marathon will inspire you to dig deep and keep going.
Albatross ULTRAIL 2025 | ITRA-Certified & UTMB Index Race
 Khagrachhari, Bangladesh**

 24-25 October 2025
Are you ready to make history? The Albatross ULTRAIL is bringing the ultimate trail running experience to Bangladesh! For the first time, local and international runners can earn ITRA points and secure a UTMB Index, paving the way to the world's most prestigious Ultra-Trail races.
 Take on the Challenge:

 33KM Challenge – 890m elevation gain | The perfect introduction to trail running.

 50KM Endurance Test – 1340m elevation gain | Push your limits and test your resilience.

 83KM Ultimate Ultra – 2230m elevation gain | A test of grit, endurance, and adventure!
★Why This Race is Special:

 ITRA Certified – Earn ITRA points for global ranking & future race qualifications.

 UTMB Index Race – Gain a UTMB Index & increase your chances for UTMB World Series entry!

 Scenic & Challenging – Run through the breathtaking hills of Khagrachhari.

 Be a Part of History – Bangladesh’s first step into the global ultra-trail community.
Your Journey to UTMB Starts Here! The Albatross ULTRAIL is your gateway to the legendary UTMB Mont-Blanc —the ultimate dream for ultra-runners worldwide!
Stay Updated & Follow Us on Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/albatrossultrail
Keep training, stay focused, and get ready to conquer the trails like never before!
