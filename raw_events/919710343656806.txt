Ramadan 7.5k CHALLENGE (season 2)
Date: 27th February, 2026. (Friday)
Event Type: Live/Virtual.
Virtual Event Details:
Record your run/walk any-day from [ 5th Ramadan 2026 - 15th Ramadan 2026] via most popular run/walk/ activity app from play store or App Store, (preferably Strava) and submit a Screenshot using submission link. Submission link will be provided 1 week before event. Also, one needs to submit the result in our group
Cut off time: None.
You need to complete 7.5 km in minimum 1 and a maximum of 2 attempts within the SAME day of your activity.
The person with the maximum Kms will be the winner of this event within this timeframe. However, if you come second, don't worry. We will be giving away prize money for the top 3 performers.
[We will be providing gifts and add category depending on the registration.*]
Activity type: Preferably outdoor, indoors are accepted as you finish 7.5 km successfully. (excluding pause time).
Our trained team of fitness activists and enthusiasts will verify your run/walk; thus your medal and belongings will be delivered to your house using the given details and number ( you can collect free of charge from our collection point otherwise delivery fee personal via Sundarban Courier Service to your home address) according to your registration type [regular/late].
