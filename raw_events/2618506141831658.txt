𝗔𝗰𝗰𝗼𝘂𝗻𝘁𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗗𝗮𝘆 𝗥𝘂𝗻 𝟮𝟬𝟮𝟱
Fitness Meets Accounting
On 𝟳𝘁𝗵 𝗡𝗼𝘃𝗲𝗺𝗯𝗲𝗿 𝟮𝟬𝟮𝟱, the streets of 𝗛𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗿𝗷𝗵𝗲𝗲𝗹 will come alive with energy as running enthusiasts, Chartered Accountants, Finance Professionals, University Students and running athletes from running communities will unite for the Accounting Day Run – a celebration of discipline, precision and endurance, values we cherish both in accounting and in running.
𝗘𝘃𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝗗𝗲𝘁𝗮𝗶𝗹𝘀:
Date: 7th November 2025
Venue: Hatirjheel, Dhaka
Reporting Time: 5:30 AM
7.5K Race Start: 6:00 AM (Tentative)
1K Race Start: 6:10 AM (Tentative)
𝗞𝗶𝘁 𝗘𝘅𝗽𝗼 𝗗𝗲𝘁𝗮𝗶𝗹𝘀:
Date: 6th November 2025
Venue: Pan Pacific Sonargaon Hotel, Dhaka
Time: 1:00 PM to 8:00 PM (Tentative)
𝗥𝗲𝗴𝗶𝘀𝘁𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗙𝗲𝗲𝘀:
• 7.5K - 800 BDT
• 1K - 600 BDT
*𝗥𝗲𝗴𝗶𝘀𝘁𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝘄𝗶𝗹𝗹 𝗯𝗲 𝗼𝗽𝗲𝗻 𝗳𝗿𝗼𝗺 𝟯𝗿𝗱 𝗢𝗰𝘁𝗼𝗯𝗲𝗿 𝟮𝟬𝟮𝟱

𝟳.𝟱𝗞 𝗖𝗵𝗮𝗹𝗹𝗲𝗻𝗴𝗲:
• CA Members (Elite & Amateur) - 300 slots
• CA Students and General - 900 slots
𝟭𝗞 𝗙𝘂𝗻 𝗥𝘂𝗻:
• Kids and Mom - 200 slots
𝗖𝘂𝘁-𝗼𝗳𝗳 𝘁𝗶𝗺𝗲:
• 7.5K - 1 hour 30 minutes
• 1K - No Cut-off time for finishing, but race time is limited to 1 hour
𝗣𝗮𝗿𝘁𝗶𝗰𝗶𝗽𝗮𝗻𝘁𝘀 𝗘𝗻𝘁𝗶𝘁𝗹𝗲𝗺𝗲𝗻𝘁𝘀:
• Foreign Made Finisher Medal
• Official Event T-Shirt
• Personal BIB with Chip Timing (Only for 7.5K)
• Goody Bag
• Silicone Wristband
• Hydration Support
• Post-Run Snacks
• Medical Aid
• E-Certificate
• Event Photography

The top 3 male and female runners in each category of 7.5K will receive prize money and a crest.

- Only registered participants will be able to join the event.
- The event is open to both local and international runners.
- Finishers must complete within the designated cut-off to receive medals.
- This is a Live Event. No Virtual!
- No BIB or registration transfers allowed.
- No refund policy.
- You are strongly discouraged from participating if you have any medical issues.
- Organizers reserve the right to make adjustments if needed.
