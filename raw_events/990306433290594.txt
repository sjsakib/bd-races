AKIJ Bicycle COX'S BAZAR MARATHON 2025!
Following the successful organization of the first Cox’s Bazar Run in 2023, the Victory Run in 2024, and the second edition Cox's Bazar Run 2025 along with the Boishakhi Triathlon in 2025 — the Cox’s Bazar Runners Community is now proudly bringing you the Cox’s Bazar Marathon 2025!
Set for November 1, 2025, this international-standard marathon invites runners from around the world to experience an unforgettable race through the stunning landscapes of Cox’s Bazar — from the vibrant town and rolling hills to the breathtaking Marine Drive, with the endless blue of the Bay of Bengal by your side.
Come run where the mountains meet the sea!
